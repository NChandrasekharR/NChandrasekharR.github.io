The Solution

We redesigned the dashboard around three core principles: Priority-First Design, Progressive Disclosure, and Actionable Insights.

üéØ Priority-First Design

The new dashboard presents information in order of urgency and importance. Teachers see their most critical tasks first, with less important information accessible through intuitive navigation.

Key Learnings

This project reinforced several important principles about designing for complex user workflows:

Design Insights

Context is everything: The same information can be overwhelming or helpful depending on when and how it's presented.

Progressive disclosure works: Starting simple and allowing users to drill down reduces cognitive load while maintaining access to detailed information.

Process Insights

Involve users early and often: Our most valuable insights came from observing teachers in their actual work environment.

Measure behavioral change: Looking beyond task completion to understand how design changes daily workflows.
